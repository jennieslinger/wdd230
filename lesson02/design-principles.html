Visual Heirarchy

Hyer Aviation

This website for Hyer Avation shows the design principle of Visual Heirarchy. The eye is initially drawn to the upper left corner to the company name then down to the middle of the page to the air plane. After the plane, the eye is drawn to the words at the bottom right hand corner. Ultimately directing the view of the page in a decending diagonal direction.

Alignment

Lacoste Heritage

This website for Lacoste Heritage shows the design principle of Alignment. The multiple number of image tiles are the same size and are aligned on the page vertically and horizontally.

Contrast

Mubasic

This website for Mubasic shows the design principle of Contrast. The left half of the loads into a muted mustard color with black block text. The eye is drawn to the black text because of the choice of background color. Alternatively, the right side of the page has a black background and a series of colored rectangles. The black background enhances the shape of the rectangles to mimic the look of keys of piano.
